Rescue Road Trips

Rescue Dog Transport

A Low Cost Transport service for Rescue Dogs in the Deep South and an Ohio based rescue. We are a licensed USDA Class T Carrier, providing loving, humane road trips to homeless, unwanted and unloved dogs facing assured-immediate death from Southern Kill Shelters. We deliver to Loving "Forever Homes" in New England and surrounding areas.

Process to adopt a pet

Finding the Right Recue Animal for You and Your Home.

Pet Selection

Do your research on breed characteristics, make sure you choose the right breed/mix for your household

Pet Meeting

Choose an animal that comes to the front of the kennel and is excited to see you, interact with it outside the shelter environment, look for soft body language; avoid an animal that is very still and alert

Filling out Forms

Make sure you understand ALL of the requirements that you need to fulfill when adopting the animal, such as spay/neuter requirements, before signing the contract

Taking Your Pet Home

Before entering your pet's new home, it should meet any other animals outside of the house, also make sure it has available water, food, a bed and even a toy
